Where did it all started?

Our company was founded by Sharie D. Llame and Krystian Ernest Ferl M. Esguerra on December 19, 2023. It all started when these two couple got an idea from their entrepeneur subject in school. Along their performance task in enterpreneur subject, they are tasked to sell something and make a profit with it, and their group comes up with an idea to sell crinkles. This inspired the two CEO to continue selling crinkles and later on, they manage to produce other goods like butternut, cookies, brownies, and more.
